Mô tả công việc

We’re looking for an HR Manager. This role is equivalent to a founding HR position and reports directly to the board of executives. Our team is young, fast-growing, and made up of 60+ curious, hardworking individuals. As we scale, we’re looking for someone to help build a strong, people-first culture and lay the foundation for a high-performing organization. You’ll lead both strategy and execution, from owning core HR systems like onboarding, performance management, internal communications, and skills training. You’ll also manage compensation and benefits, ensure compliance with labor laws, and implement reliable systems for maintaining employee data. This is a rare opportunity to build from the ground up. We’re looking for a hands-on leader who brings structure to ambiguity, listens well, and leads by doing, not by pointing fingers. If you excel in fast-moving environments, can bring clarity to chaos, and know how to build lean, practical solutions that actually fit a startup’s needs, we’d love to meet you. Bachelor’s degree in Human Resources, Business Administration, or a related field In-depth knowledge of Vietnam’s labor laws, personal income tax regulations, and social insurance policies 5–7 years of HR generalist experience, including at least 3 years in a managerial role Proven ability to source and hire senior and leadership-level talent Solid understanding of recruiting channels and talent markets, particularly in Hanoi Experience in ecommerce tech startups is a strong plus Skilled in using workflow and collaboration tools such as Lark Suite Experience with HRIS, ATS, or other HR technologies is a plus, but not required Demonstrated experience building and executing employer branding strategies Excellent interpersonal and collaboration skills, with the ability to influence stakeholders across all levels High emotional intelligence and sound judgment, with the discretion to handle sensitive matters Strong business acumen, analytical thinking, and negotiation skills.

Competitive salary and performance-based incentives 12 days of paid annual leave 13th-month salary bonus Team-building activities and company events Personal laptop provided Additional benefits in accordance with company policies and Vietnam Labor Law.
